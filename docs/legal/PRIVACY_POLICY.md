## Introduction

Roomate ("we", "us") operates the Roomate mobile application (the "App"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the App.

## Information we collect

- **Account information**: name, email address, and password (stored securely; password is hashed).
- **Household data**: household names, locations you choose to enter, shared expenses, shopping lists, events, chores, and related content you create in the App.
- **Profile data**: profile photo if you upload one.
- **Device / technical data**: push notification tokens if you enable notifications; basic device info as needed to run the App.
- **Location**: only if you use features that request location (e.g. suggesting an address), as described in the system permission prompt.

We do not sell your personal information.

## How we use information

We use the information to:

- Provide and maintain accounts and household features.
- Send transactional emails (e.g. verification) and, if enabled, push notifications about activity you care about.
- Improve reliability and security of the service.

## Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

If applicable, we rely on **contract** (providing the service), **legitimate interests** (security, improvement), and **consent** where required (e.g. optional notifications or location).

## Sharing

We may use service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g. hosting, database, email delivery). We require them to protect your information. We may disclose information if required by law.

## Data retention

We retain your information while your account is active and as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account in the App under **Settings → Account → Delete account** (you will be asked to confirm with your password). You can also email us at the address below to ask about deletion or export.

## Security

We use industry-standard measures appropriate to the nature of the service. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## International transfers

If you use the App from outside the country where you live, your information may be processed in the **United States** and in other countries where we or our service providers operate (including where our API, database, and email providers host data). We use appropriate safeguards where required by law.

## Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, or to object to certain processing. Contact us at **support.roomateapp@gmail.com** to exercise these rights.

## Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version at this URL and update the "Last updated" date.
