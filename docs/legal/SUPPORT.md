# Support (template for App Store)

App Store Connect requires a **support URL**. Publish one of the following (or similar) at a public HTTPS page and use that URL in the listing.

## Short support page copy

**Roomate help**

For questions, bug reports, or account issues, contact us at:

**Email:** [YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL@example.com]

We aim to respond within [2–5] business days.

---

Optional sections you can add on the same page:

- **Account deletion**: Explain how users can delete their account (e.g. in-app path: Settings → Danger zone → Delete account), if applicable.
- **FAQ**: Link to a doc or expand with common questions.

## What you need to do

1. Replace the placeholder email with one you monitor.
2. Host this content (GitHub Pages, your domain, Notion public page, etc.).
3. Paste the **public URL** into App Store Connect as the Support URL.
